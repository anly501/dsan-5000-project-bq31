Research Introduction

Welcome to the introduction section of our research project. In this section, we will provide an overview of our research topic, its significance and academic citations.

Topic Overview

Our research focuses on Steam game sales and marketing.

Relevance and Importance

Understanding steam game sale and marketing is crucial for a steam-game business to increase revenues.

Research Goals and Hypotheses

  1. What is the impact steam game reviews?
  2. What is the best marketing approach base on game production timeline?
  3. What are the top sale games in the past 5 years?
  4. What marketing factors are in common from those top sales?
  5. How will customer feel like the game published on different dates?
  6. What kind of broadcasting platform is the best?
  7. How do Youtube game blogger views related to sales?
  8. Should game business continue marketing after game published?
  9. How likely a non-related customer being targeted from marketing?
  10. What discount should business set when publishing?

These questions will guide our research and help us understand the significance.
